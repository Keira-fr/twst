About Pomefiore

Welcome to Pomefiore! A dorm based on the Fairest Queen's spirit of tenacity. Twisted from Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs.

Pomefiore is the oldest of the dorms and houses students who excel at potionology and curses, as well as cultivating a unique sense of style.
