About Ignihyde

Welcome to Ignihyde! A dorm based on the King of the Underworld's spirit of diligence. Twisted from Hercules.

Ignihyde is a home to students who specialize in non-magical technology, giving the dorm a distinctive atmosphere. The dorm is occupied by students who excel in tech-related fields, as well as magical-energy engineers, focused on researching the integration of magic with the latest technology. Ignihyde students tend to be quiet, serious, and not very outgoing.
